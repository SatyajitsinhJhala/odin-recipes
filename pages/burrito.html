Burrito Gigantesco

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can of Rosarita Baked Beans

  • 1 Tortilla

Serves: 1

Directions:

  1. Take can of Rosarita Baked Beans on a flat and level surface and with a can opener open the beans.

  2. Open the bag of Tortillas and place on a clean countertop.

  3. Poke a hole at the bottom of the can of baked beans.

  4. Turn the can upside down over the Tortilla and tap to release the beans.

  5. Orient the beans such that they lay horizontally on the Tortilla.

  6. Wrap the Tortilla around the beans.
