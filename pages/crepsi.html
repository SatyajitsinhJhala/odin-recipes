Crepsi en Baguette

Ingredients:

  • 1 Crab

  • 1 Baguette

  • 1 bottle of 811 Chateau d'Yquem

  • 2 liters of Pepsi Max

Serves: 2

Directions:

  1. Prepare the crab

    • Start by ensuring the crab is fresh and alive. Clean it thoroughly by removing any dirt or impurities.

  2. Boil the Pepsi Max

    • Pour the 2 liters of Pepsi Max into a large pot and bring it to a boil.

  3. Add the crab

    • Gently place the live crab into the boiling Pepsi Max. Be careful to avoid getting splashed.

  4. Season the boiling liquid

    • Add salt and pepper to taste to the boiling Pepsi Max. This will help infuse flavor into the crab.

  5. Simmer for 2 hours

    • Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the crab cook in the Pepsi Max for 2 hours. Ensure the crab is fully submerged in the liquid.

  6. Remove the crab

    • Carefully remove the crab from the pot using tongs or a slotted spoon. Place it on a plate or cutting board.

  7. Prepare the baguette

    • Slice the baguette lengthwise, creating a pocket for the crab. Optionally, you can lightly toast the baguette for added texture.

  8. Assemble and serve

    • Place the cooked crab inside the baguette, adding any desired toppings like mayonnaise, lettuce, or sliced tomatoes. Serve the crab-filled baguette while it's still warm.Serve with a glass of 811 Chateau d'Yquem
