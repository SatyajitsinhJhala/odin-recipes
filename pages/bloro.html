Blörö

Ingredients:

  • Coffee

  • Vodka

  • Cigarette

Serves: 1

Directions:

  1. Prepare the coffee

    • Start by boiling the water then adding coffee to it the pouring into a cup.

  2. Vodka

    • Pour the Vodka into a shot glass.

  3. Cigarette

    • Gently open the pack of cigarettes and remove a singular cigarette.

  4. Plating

    • Place the coffee on the plate followed by the vodka and then the cigarette.
